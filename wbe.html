Textual Resources

Whole Brain Emulation: A Roadmap The fundamental and legendary paper spurring directed efforts. Sandberg and Bostrum.

The Age of Em: Work, Love and Life when Robots Rule the Earth Robin Hanson (2016). This influential book, encyclopedic in scope, explores a future society dominated EMs and delves into the economic, social, and ethical implications of widespread whole brain emulation.

A Taxonomy and Metaphysics of Mind-Uploading Keith Wiley. A detailed and passionate exploration of the philosophical and metaphysical issues surrounding mind-uploading.

Technical Resources: Structure to Function

FlyWire: Collaborative ConnectomicsFlywire, most papers using the connectome listed at the bottom of the page.

Predicting Visual Function by Interpreting a Neuronal Wiring Diagram H.S. Seung (2024). The first major effort at predicting neuronal function from structural data in visual systems.

Neuronal Parts List and Wiring Diagram for a Visual System Matsliah A. et al. (2024). A comprehensive look at the achievements of FlyWire in deciphering visual system structures.

Interneuron Diversity and Normalization Specificity in a Visual System H. Sebastian Seung (2024). A research paper on interneuron diversity and its role in normalization

Technical Resources: Neuronal Interaction to Behaviour

The Time is Ripe to Reverse Engineer an Entire Nervous System Keith Wiley (2024). A state-of-the-art overview of C. Elegans simulations and the path forward for understanding neural behavior.

Technical Resources: WBE Overviews

Distillation of Neurotech and Alignment Workshop January 2023 Lisa Thiergart, Sumner L. Norman (2023). A summary of discussions on neurotech alignment challenges.

Technical Resources: Connectomics

AI Speeding up EM Image Acquisition for Connectomics Yaron Meirovitch et al. (2023). This paper demonstrates a significant acceleration in image acquisition for connectomics.

Technical Resources: NeuroAI

NeuroAI Course by Neuromatch Academy A comprehensive introduction to NeuroAI, blending neuroscience and artificial intelligence concepts, with tutorials designed to bridge the two fields for aspiring researchers and professionals.

Technical Resources: Expansion Microscopy (ExM)

Expansion Microscopy: Principles and Uses in Biological Research Asmamaw T. Wassie, Yongxin Zhao & Edward S. Boyden (2019). A comprehensive paper discussing the principles and applications of expansion microscopy in biological research, showcasing its transformative potential in resolving nanoscale structures.

Philosophical Resources: Personal Identity and Transhumanism

Transhumanism and Personal Identity James Hughes (2013). A vital exploration of personal identity issues in the context of WBE and transhumanism.

Philosophical Resources: Branching Identity

Nondestructive Mind Uploading and the Stream of Consciousness Keith Wiley (2023). A deep dive into the philosophical implications of branching identity and mind uploading.

Philosophical Resources: Ethics in WBE

Ethics of Brain Emulations Anders Sandberg (2014). This paper explores the ethical implications of brain emulation, addressing issues such as identity, rights, and moral status in a world where whole brain emulation is possible.
